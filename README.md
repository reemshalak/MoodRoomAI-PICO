## Overview
**MoodRoom AI** is a Mixed Reality (MR) experience that transforms your real living space based on your emotions or desired atmosphere. By scanning your room and describing how you want it to feel, AI generates personalized environmental enhancements such as lighting, colors, and ambience.

Instead of imagining how a room might look, users can **see changes directly in their real space through a headset**, creating an immersive and interactive design experience.

MoodRoom AI blends **Mixed Reality and Artificial Intelligence** to turn interior inspiration into a visual, interactive experience.

## Concept
The core idea is simple:

1. **Scan your room**
2. **Describe your mood or desired vibe**
3. **AI generates enhancements**
4. **See the transformation instantly in Mixed Reality**

The system highlights why each suggestion fits the mood, creating a **purposeful and personalized room transformation**.

No renovation required — just imagination and immersion.

## How It Works
1. The user scans their environment using the MR headset.
2. The system maps the room and identifies spatial surfaces.
3. The user describes a mood or atmosphere.
4. AI generates enhancement suggestions.
5. The environment updates visually in real-time.
